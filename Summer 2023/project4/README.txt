How to compile:

Must be in the child directory of "cs1302" and "project4". "cs1302" --> "project4" --> "mavenshapes"
Once within the "mavenshapes" directory, input:

"mvn compile"
to compile the program


How to test:

within the same directory and after compiling, input:

"mvn test"
to test the program. There should be a total of 15 tests.
